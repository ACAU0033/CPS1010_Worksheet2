Fettucino Al Fredo

•	24 ounces dry fettuccini pasta
•	1 cup butter
•	¾  pint heavy cream
•	salt and pepper to taste
•	1 dash garlic salt
•	¾ cup granted Romano cheese
•	½ cup granted Parmesan cheese
Recipe:
1) Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add fettucini and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain
2) In a large saucepan, melt better into cream over low heat. Add salt, pepper and garlic salt. Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted; this sill thicken the sauce.
3) Add pasta to sauce. Use enopugh of the paste so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
